Verbum Disco: How to play


At first, you will have to upload the .txt file with words and their translations in the form. The word and translated word in .txt file have to be in a one line separated by semi-column and 1 space. Each 2-tuple of words have to be in new line. At least 5 2-tuples of words should be added.

The set of the words will be stored and after clicking DONE button, the game will start.
Then you will be asked to translate {{ no_of_selected}} randomly chosen words from the set of the words you uploaded. After each right translation, you will gain points. At the end, you will maybe see the summary.

To start the game, click above on 'Verbum Disco game'.
