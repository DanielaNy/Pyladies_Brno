Verbum Disco: How to play


At first, you will have to the .txt file with words and their translations in the form. The word and translated word in .txt file have to be in one line separated by semi-column and 1 space. Each 2-tuple of words have to be in new line. Please, ommit empty lines.

See the example: EXAMPLE .txt FILE

The set of the words will be stored and after clicking DONE button, the game will start.
Then you will be asked to translate 10 randomly chosen words from the set of the words you uploaded. After each right translation, you will gain points. At the end, you will maybe see the summary.
